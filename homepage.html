ABOUT US

"IKIGAI" is japanese secret of happy life that comes from two word iki meaning “alive” or “life,” and gai, meaning “benefit” or “worth.” This simple website intends to help individuals to simplify their lives by pursuing what sparks joy for them by simply combining learnings of the book in the practical form.

The website tries to combine learnings of ikigai book in the form of small article that provides insights of the thoughts and esence of how ikigai tries to allowing the self’s possibilities to blossom.And, this treasure map can help you find your way to finding wonderful things about yourself that you can share with the world, and the world will say ‘thank you’ for it.”

“Ikigai is what, day after day and year after year, each of us most essentially lives for.”
